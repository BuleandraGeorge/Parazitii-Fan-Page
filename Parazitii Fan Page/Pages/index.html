Parazitii

Who are they?

Paraziții (Romanian for "The Parasites", pronounced [paraˈzit͡sij]) is a Romanian three-man rap and hip hop group founded in 1994.
It consists of Cheloo (Cătălin Ștefan Ion), Ombladon (Bogdan Ionuț Pastaca), and FreakaDaDisk (Petre Urda).

Members

Cheloo

Cheloo (Cătălin Ștefan Ion by his real name) is a Romanian hip-hop artist and a founding member of Paraziții. He is also a music producer and together with Ombladon and Freakadadisk he set up 20 CM Records.
He started out as a producer and sound engineer at Studio Box, the place where he gained most of his experience. Starting 2003 he has his own studio and is currently promoting his personal label called 16HZ and acts as a producer for two local acts.

Ombladon

Ombladon (Bogdan Ionuț Păstacă by his real name) is Romanian hip-hop artist that in 1994 join Cheloo to form the hip-hop band, Parazitii. The first recorded song is "In Jur" and appeared on September 13, 1994. A day later they held their first concert, at a high-school prom. On April 10, 2004 Ombladon launches its first album, Condoleanțe, album awarded with the Golden Disc for the large number of copies sold.

FreakaDaDisk

FreakaDaDisk (Petre Urda by his real name) is Romanian hip-hop artist member of Paraziții from 1999.He has been a member of the Morometze band since 1997. Later in 1999, he decide to leave the band and colaborates with Parazitii in album "Nici o Problema". After this colaboration, he joins in the band.
